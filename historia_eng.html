-- OUR STORY --

Legend has it that the pirate "Red-leg" Zeff built a floating restaurant, the Baratie, a neutral place where pirates and sailors could eat without being afraid of enemy attacks.

According to the legend, commodore Don Krieg attacked the Baratie to claim the restaurant's food supplies, however, he had to face "Strawhat" Luffy and his crew, who managed to defeat him and kept Baratie safe.

From that moment on, the Baratie sailed all over the seas, providing food to countless pirates.

Based on this legend, the Baratie, our own floating restaurant, sails all over the world, providing food to people all around the globe.

In the wake of Magallanes y Elcano, the Baratie sails following the trail of these skilled sailors, passing through countless cities, countries and continents. During our voyage, we carry out stays in these cities, allowing local residents to pay a visit to our restaurant, alongside allowing home delivery, which is carried out by our crew.
